A Werewolf?

Pool Health: 4
“Is Meat”: "15"
“Has no Holes”: "10"
“Once a Human”: "5"
“On the Hunt”: "8"

Bloodied: At 2 Pool Health this enemy "gains 3 attacks but will use 2 a turn on destroying the environment around it and fleeing"

Distracted: "This enemy is distracted by memories, scents, and sounds"

Vicious: Attacks Twice

____________________________________________

Horrid Bite: Roll Fight, On a success "takes no damage but one point is moved out of Violence", on a failure "take 3 Vigil damage" if your violence is above 4 take "2 Vigil" damage if it is below 4 take "5 Vigil damage", then "move 3 points out of violence". This enemy then becomes ‘bloated’ "and all forced rolls it makes any player make are rolled as if pushed."

Muddy Claws: Roll Security, "On a success, take 1 Vigil, on a failure distribute 3–5 Vigil damage between yourself and a fellow teammate"

Shifting Hair: Roll Secrets, "On a failure whenever you act you take 1 Grasp damage, this effect stacks, another player may remove this effect from you entirely with a successful Secrets roll as they explain the patterns of how the hair moves in unsettling ways."
